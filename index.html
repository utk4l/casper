Frequently asked question

Still do you have any questions to know?
1. What does CASPER stands for?
CASPER stands for Career Advancement Selection Recruitment and Educational Recruitment. This evidently states that we assist students with entire process which starts from career discussion, selection of the best university, Assistance in Education Loan, Visa and Accommodation support and what not.
2. Do you offer complete solution for students?
Yes, we provide a complete solution for students, end-to-end assistance and hand holding through out the process.
3. Which country is safe and better for higher study?
Casper basically recruits students in various countries like CANADA, AUSTRALIA, UK, IRELAND, SINGAPORE, DUBAI, NEW ZEALAND, United States of America, etc. These all English Speaking countries and are safe with student-friendly environment.
4. Which country offer PR after study getting job?
Countries like Canada, Australia, and New Zealand offer PR pathways after completing studies and securing a job in their respective countries. The specific requirements and processes may vary, so thorough research is essential.
5. Can i get scholarship with my low CGPA?
Securing a scholarship abroad with a low CGPA is challenging but not impossible. We assist students with scholarships but huge amount of scholarships are meant for deserving students. Students with low GPA get Grants and Bursaries which depends on different institutions.
6. Do you allow accomadation for students in abroad?
Yes, we do help in accommodations for the respective countries we recruit in. We have tie ups with various accommodation partners like University Living, Amber, etc. We help students with budget friendly accommodations .
